Aaron Draplin

Aaron Draplin is the Sole proprietor of Draplin Design Co.  He is a Graphic Designer, author, and entrepreneur based in Portland, Oregon.  He has made designs and logos for Nike, Target, Ford Motor Company, NASA/JPL, Patagonia, Nixon Watches and even the Obama Administration. He graduated from the Minneapolis College of Art + Design in December 2000.  He has been featured on TED Talks and talks at Google.  In 2019 he even designed a US postal stamp, Star Ribbon.
